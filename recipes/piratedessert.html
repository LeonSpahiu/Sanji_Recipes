Pirates Dessert

Ingredients

  • Good quality ice cream
  • Chocolate sauce
  • Fruit: fresh or tinned fruit salad, sliced bananas, fresh strawberries, mango cheeks.
  • Chopped nuts or sprinkles
  • Ice cream wafers

Steps

  1. Layer ice cream, fruit and chocolate sauce in a bowl or cup.

  2. Top with nuts or sprinkles.

  3. Grab a spoon and enjoy!
